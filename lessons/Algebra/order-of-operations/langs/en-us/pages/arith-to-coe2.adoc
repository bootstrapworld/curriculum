= Drawing the Structure 2

For each arithmetic expression on the left, draw its Circle of Evaluation on the right.

@do{
  (define expr1 '(- (+ 6 17) -2))
  (define expr2 '(* (+ 2 17) (- 12 8)))
  (define expr3 '(* (* 23 14) (+ 3 20)))
  (define expr4 '(+ (- 5 17) (* 14 5)))
}
