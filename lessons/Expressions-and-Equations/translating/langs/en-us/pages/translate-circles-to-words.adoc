= Translating from Circles of Evaluation to Words

Translate each Circle of Evaluation into words. The first one is done for you.

[.FillVerticalSpace, cols="^.^1a,^.^10a,^.^10a",options="header",stripes="none"]
|===
|    | Circle of Evaluation 			| Expression in Words
|@n| @show{(coe '(- 35 8))}				| the difference of 35 and 8
|@n| @show{(coe '(/ (- 35 8) 2))}		| @ifsoln{half the difference of 35 and 8}
|@n| @show{(coe '(- 54 (- 35 8)))}		| @ifsoln{54 decreased by the difference bof 35 and 8}
|@n| @show{(coe '(* 10 (- 35 8)))}		| @ifsoln{the product of 10 and the difference of 35 and 8}
|@n| @show{(coe '(- (- 35 8) 9))}		| @ifsoln{9 less than the difference of 35 and 8}
|@n| @show{(coe '(* 4 (- 35 8)))}		| @ifsoln{the product of 4 and the difference of 35 and 8}
|@n| @show{(coe '(+ (- 35 8) 2))}		| @ifsoln{add 2 to the difference of 35 and 8}
|===
