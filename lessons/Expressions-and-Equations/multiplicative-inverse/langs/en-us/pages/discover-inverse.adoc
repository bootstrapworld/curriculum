== From Division to Multiplying by Fractions

@n Scooter claims that *dividing by an integer is equivalent to multiplying by the reciprocal unit fraction* and and draws the Circles of Evaluation below as proof. Translate the Circles of Evaluation into expressions and compute their values. 

[cols="5,1,5", stripes="none", frame="none", grid="none"]
|===
^.^| @show{(coe '(/ 12 4))}
| @arrow  
^.^|@show{(coe  '(* 12 1/4))}           
|===

@hspace{10em}@math{12 \div 4 =} @fitb{5em}{@ifsoln{3}}
@hspace{20em}
one quarter of 12 is@fitb{5em}{@ifsoln{3}}
