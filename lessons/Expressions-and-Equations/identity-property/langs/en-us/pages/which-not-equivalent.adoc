@show{(coe '(* 1 (* 13 4)))}

| @ifsoln{The 2nd Circle of Evaluation starts with 1 and divides, resulting in @math{\frac{1}{(13 \times 4)}}, rather than @math{13 \times 4}.}

| @ifsoln{The 2nd Circle of Evaluation multiplies by 0, so will result in zero.}
