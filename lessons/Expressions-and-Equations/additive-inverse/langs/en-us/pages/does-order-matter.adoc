For each expression, we've drawn two unique Circles of Evaluation.

- The Order of Operations tell us that we can always evaluate addition and subtraction from left to right, but sometimes we get the same answer when we don't work from left to right. Can you find a pattern?
- Evaluate each pair of Circles of Evaluation below. Determine whether or not they are equivalent.

[.FillVerticalSpace,cols="^.^1a,^.^3a,^.^6a,^.^5a, 2a", stripes="none", options="header"]
|===
|	 
| Expression 
| Adding & Subtracting from Left-to-Right +
ALWAYS Gives us the Correct Answer 
| How about Right-to-Left?
| Equivalent?

| @n
| @math{20 + 8 - 5}
| @show{(coe '(- (+ 20 8) 5))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{23}}}
| @show{(coe '(+ 20 (- 8 5)))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{23}}}
| @ifsoln{Yes}

| @n
| @math{4 + 3 - 2}
| @show{(coe '(- (+ 4 3) 2))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{5}}}
| @show{(coe '(+ 4 (- 3 2)))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{5}}}
| @ifsoln{Yes}

| @n
| @math{12 - 9 + 8}
| @show{(coe '(+ (- 12 9) 8))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{11}}}
| @show{(coe '(- 12 (+ 9 8)))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{5}}}
| @ifsoln{No}

| @n
| @math{64 + 92 - 91}
| @show{(coe '(- (+ 64 92) 91))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{65}}}
| @show{(coe '(+ 64 (- 92 91)))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{65}}}
| @ifsoln{Yes}

| @n
| @math{120 - 13 + 7}
| @show{(coe '(+ (- 120 13) 7))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{114}}}
| @show{(coe '(- 120 (+ 13 7)))} = @fitb{2em}{@ifsoln{@math{100}}}
| @ifsoln{No}
|===
