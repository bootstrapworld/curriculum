== From Adding to Subtracting

@n Kelli claims that *adding a negative is the same as subtracting a positive* and and draws the Circles of Evaluation below as proof. Translate the Circles of Evaluation into expressions and compute their values. 

[cols="5,1,5", stripes="none"]
|===
^.^|@show{(coe  '(+ 10 -3))}             
| @arrow  
^.^| @show{(coe '(- 10 3))}

| @ifsoln{@math{10 + -3 = 7}}
| @arrow  
| @ifsoln{@math{10 - 3 = 7}}
|===

@n Are these expressions equivalent?    @ifsoln-choice{Yes}    No
