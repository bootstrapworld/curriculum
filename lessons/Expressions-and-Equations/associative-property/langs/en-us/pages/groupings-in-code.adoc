== *Do groupings matter for @show{(code 'beside)}?* 

@n Make a prediction, will the two sets of instructions below produce the same image? @fitb{}{}

- Place an orange dot to the right of an aqua star, then place a red star to the right of them.
- Make an image that places a red star to to the right of an orange dot. Place them to the right of an aqua star.

@vspace{1ex}

Let's find out. Translate the two Circles of Evaluation into code, test your code in @proglang and make a sketch of what @proglang returns.
