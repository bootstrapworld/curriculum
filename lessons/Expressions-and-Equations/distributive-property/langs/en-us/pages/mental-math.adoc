= The Distributive Property and Mental Math

On this page, the goal is to learn to see expressions flexibly to *make the math easier* to solve in our heads. 

- Complete each row, by drawing two equivalent Circles of Evaluation - one in factored form and one in expanded form. 
- Choose the expression that would be easier to evaluate in your head and place a checkmark next to it.
- Then compute the answer.
