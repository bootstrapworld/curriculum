Look at the two "sideways" data tables below. 

[cols="^.^1a,^.^1a", frame="none"]
|===
|

[.sideways-pyret-table]

! y !   ! 2 ! 3 ! 4 ! 5 ! 6 !
!===

|

[.sideways-pyret-table]
!===
! x !   !   1 !   2 !   3 !   4 !   5 !
! y !   ! -12 ! -14 ! -16 ! -18 ! -20 !
!===

|===

[cols="1,1"]
|===
|What do you *Notice?*
|What do you *Wonder?*
|@vspace{45ex}
|@vspace{45ex}
|===

* What would the next x,y pair for each of the tables?

* What would the y-value for each table be when x is 0?
