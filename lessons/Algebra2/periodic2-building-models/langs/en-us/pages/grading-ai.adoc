An AI was asked to fit a periodic model to each of the scatter plots below. What did the AI get right? What did it get wrong?

[.FillVerticalSpace, cols="3a,4a", frame="none", options="noheader", stripes="none"]
|===
| @image{../images/fn_sin-1-8th-pi.png}
@center{*AI's Suggested Model: @math{f(x) = 1.5\sin(\frac{\pi}{8} \times x)}*}
| 
[cols = "3a,2a,2a,2a,4a", options="header"]
!===
! 			    ! midline 		! amplitude 	! period 		! horizontal shift
! scatter plot  ! @ifsoln{0}	!@ifsoln{1}		! @ifsoln{16}	! 				
! AI-model		! @ifsoln{0}	!@ifsoln{1.5}   ! @ifsoln{16}	!				
! got it right? ! @ifsoln{Y}	!@ifsoln{N} 	! @ifsoln{Y}	!				
!===

What information was the AI missing? @fitb{}{}

@blanklines{1}{
@ifsoln{The AI seems to have used only the most extreme (highest and lowest) points on the scatter plot rather than sticking to the middle of the cloud. As a result, it computed the amplitude incorrectly.}
}
