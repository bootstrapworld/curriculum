== Frequency-based model: @hspace{2em} @math{f(x) = a \sin(b(x - h)) + k}

@vspace{1ex}

Periodic models can also be written in terms of their @vocab{frequency} (@math{b}). @hspace{20em} @math{b = \frac{2\pi}{P}} 

@vspace{1ex}

*The @vocab{frequency} is the number of complete cycles (from one @vocab{peak} or @vocab{valley} to another) over a fixed length of time.* +
_For example, 1 Hertz (Hz) equals one cycle per second - the frequency of a healthy human heartbeat._

@vspace{1ex}

@vocab{Frequency}-based models tend to be how engineers think about cyclical behavior.
