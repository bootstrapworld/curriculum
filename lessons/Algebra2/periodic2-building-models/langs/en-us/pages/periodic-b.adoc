^|Period-based model (@math{P})

|@vocab{Period}: _time to complete one cycle from one @vocab{peak} or @vocab{valley} to another_
|@vocab{Frequency}: _how many completed cycles happen in a length of time_

|
For example:

- It takes the moon about @fitbruby{5em}{_1 month_}{period} to orbit the earth. +
- Teenagers check their phones about every @fitbruby{8em}{_10-22 minutes_}{period}
|
For example:

- The moon orbits the earth about: @fitbruby{8em}{_12x per year_}{frequency}. +
- Teenagers check their phones about: @fitbruby{8em}{_50-100x per day_}{frequency}.
