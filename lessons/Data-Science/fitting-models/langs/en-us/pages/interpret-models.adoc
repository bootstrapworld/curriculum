== Comparing Models

@n Is Jo's model better or worse than Cy's model? @fitb{}{@ifsoln{better!}}

@n How much
@fitbruby{7em}{@ifsoln{more}}{more / less }
error do we expect in predictions made with Jo's model than predictions made with Cy's model?
