= Box Plots Formative Assessment

== Published Assessment

- Desmos File: @link{https://teacher.desmos.com/activitybuilder/custom/668d7be8fd7c6212638763d6?collections=66d8678a5c04bcca1cb0544c, Formative Assessment: Box Plots}

== Objectives

- Determine the values in the five-number summary for a dataset (minimum, Q1, median, Q3, and maximum).
- Interpret the values in the five-number summary for a dataset (minimum, Q1, median, Q3, and maximum).
- Interpret a box plot to answer questions about a dataset and its spread.
- Describe box plot shape including skew, symmetry and potential outliers.
- Identify median, interquartile range and range of datasets from box plots.
- Recognize the shape of various distributions, and identify that shape in box plots and histograms.

== Questions and Solutions

@n Click the correct 5-number summary for this dataset: 2, 3, 5, 8, 12, 20 [*A*, B, C, D]

@n You will see this 5-number summary accompany the questions on each of the next four slides. _Distribution of Sodium in 461 Fast Food Items - Min: 2.9, Q1: 35, Median: 494, Q3: 654, Max: 1140_

... The greatest amount of sodium in a fast food item in this dataset is ?? mg. [2651.9, *1140*, 654, 494]
... The interquartile range for grams of sodium is ?? mg. [1137.1, *619*, 491.1, 494]
... 25% of the values in this dataset are between 2.9 mg and ?? mg of sodium. [*35*, 348, 494, 654]
... 50% of the values in this dataset are between 1140 mg and ?? mg of sodium. [654, *494*, 351, 2.9]

@n The next five slides feature True/False questions about this box plot.

... Most of the animals were adopted in 7 weeks or less. [True]
... About 50% of the animals were adopted in 4 weeks or less. [True]
... Animals that are adopted in under two weeks are outliers. [False]
... 75% of the animals were adopted in 2.5 weeks or more. [True]
... If there are 32 animals in the shelter, then 8 animals spent at least 7 weeks at the shelter before adoption. [True]

@n Match each box plot with the correct name and description of its shape. [Skew left - there are potential outliers at the low end of the range; plot has longer whisker on the left; Skew right - there are potential outliers at the high end of the range; plot has longer whisker on the right; Symmetric - the distribution of the lower half of the data appears to mirror the distribution of the upper half of the data; both whiskers are the same length]

@n Pokemon score distributions: On the next three slides you will consider true/false statements about how the distribution of Pokemon attack scores and defense scores compare to each other.

... Both datasets have the same minimum value. [True]
... The top box plot (attack) has a greater range. [False]
... The top box plot (attack) has a greater interquartile range. [True]

@n Which of the following is correct about the box plot displayed on the left?

... The corresponding histogram will have most of the data clustered on the right.
... *The corresponding histogram will have most of the data clustered on the left.*
... The corresponding histogram will be symmetric.
... The corresponding histogram will have an even distribution - all bars equally spaced and of equal height.
