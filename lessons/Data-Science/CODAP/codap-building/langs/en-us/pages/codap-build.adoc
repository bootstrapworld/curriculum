@fitb{}{@ifsoln{It will build a column that states `true` for heavy animals and `false` for lightweight animals.}}

@fitb{}{}

@n Ensure that the collection you are adding to is "cases" (Box 5).

@vspace{1ex}

@vspace{1ex}
