@n Are the grassy images still clustered together? @fitb{4em}{@ifsoln{yes}} Do you see any _other_ clusters? @fitb{}{} +
@fitb{}{@ifsoln{The images with trees and water are clustered, and the squares are clustered with the sunset.}}
