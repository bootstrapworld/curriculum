Can you identify the Form and r-Values of these displays?

If the form is linear, approximate the  @math{r}-value to express Direction and Strength.

*Reminder:* An @math{r}-value close to -1 is a strong negative relationship, an @math{r}-value close to 0 is weak, and an @math{r}-value close to +1 is a strong positive! If the relationshipâ€™s strength is moderate, the r-value will be closer to -0.5 or +0.5.
